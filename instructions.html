DISCLAIMER

Your steam profile has to be public to work

To find your Steam Id, visit steamcommunity.com/search/users/



In the search bar, type in your Steam username or Steam alias, then select your profile from the list



With your profile loaded successfully, look at your web browser's address field.

The last part of the URL (/profiles/YourSteamId) is your Steam Id!

Make sure that it is 17 digits long
